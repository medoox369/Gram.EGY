    Egypt has wonderful beach weather throughout the year. In summer, the northern region is especially ideal, while in winter the south is the best choice. Today we are focusing on Northern Egypt: Alexandria and the North Coast. Why Alexandria? The city is only about three flight hours from Munich and is over 2,000 years old. It is one of the most beautiful cities on the Mediterranean Sea and has around 5.5 million inhabitants. In addition, there are about 20 beaches, 10 resorts, and around 190 hotels. Along the North Coast there are more than 150 resorts with fine white sandy beaches, and massive investments are currently being made there.

More than 60 international films have been shot in Egypt. No surprise—Egypt has a unique charm. We will help you understand how the ancient Egyptians built their monuments and civilization. In Egypt, you can combine a beach holiday with a cultural holiday at a very affordable price.

    Attractions and Activities in Egypt

  • Explore Cairo, one of the largest cities in the world with over 22 million inhabitants, where ancient history and modern life meet along the Nile.
  • Visit the Pyramids of Giza, the Step Pyramid of Saqqara, Dahshur, and the ancient capital Memphis to experience Egypt’s Pharaonic heritage.
  • Discover Islamic and Coptic Cairo, including the Citadel of Saladin, the Muhammad Ali Mosque, Al-Azhar Mosque, the Sultan Hassan Mosque, and historic streets such as El-Muizz and El-Darb El-Ahmar.
  • Walk through Downtown Cairo, Zamalek, Garden City, and Heliopolis and admire the Khedivial architecture inspired by the Parisian style.
  • Enjoy the Nile in Cairo, which stretches for more than 40 km through the city, with islands such as Zamalek and Roda offering unique views and experiences.
  • Experience Cairo nightlife with restaurants, cafés, theaters, the Opera House, amusement parks, and modern shopping centers.
  • Relax in Cairo’s green spaces such as Al-Azhar Park, Fustat Park, the Japanese Garden, and the Cairo Zoo.
  • Take a day trip to a traditional Egyptian village to experience authentic rural life and culture.
  • Enjoy a Nile cruise between Upper and Lower Egypt and discover cities along the riverbanks.
  • Take a boat trip through the Suez Canal and watch huge ships passing through one of the world’s most important waterways.
  • Luxor: Explore the Karnak Temple complex, the Valley of the Kings and Queens with more than 90 royal tombs, and the impressive temples of Ramses II, Hatshepsut, and Thutmose III.
  • Aswan: Visit the legendary Abu Simbel temples, Kom Ombo Temple, the beautiful Philae Temple, and also the Edfu Temple dedicated to the god Horus.
  • Sinai: A sacred land where Prophet Moses experienced great miracles. Visit Mount Sinai, the Holy Valley of Tuwa, and Wadi Al-Asal, one of the most beautiful colored valleys in the world.
